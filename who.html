We specialize in providing expert biotech consultancy services in the field of drug discovery in multiple therapeutic areas. Ganesh Iyer, PhD Ganesh has been active in the drug discovery space for over 20 years. He has valuable experience in assay development and HTS during his stints at Vertex Pharm, Warp Drive Bio, Lifemine Therapeutics and Alkermes. He was the head of Biology at a NewCo that he helped co-found at RA Ventures.
